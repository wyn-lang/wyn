## Agent Lessons Learned

**All 3 agents failed** to produce output after 4-8 hours.

**Lesson:** For complex implementation tasks, direct coding is:
- Faster (30 min vs 8 hours)
- More reliable (guaranteed output)
- Higher quality (tested immediately)

**Agents good for:** Documentation, simple examples, analysis
**Agents bad for:** Complex implementation, debugging, integration
